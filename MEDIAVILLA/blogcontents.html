"Masinag Junction"

Date: 30 April 2023

Blog #2

Masinag Junction - This route was utilized for travellers bound for Marilaque Highway, or Antipolo
Proper when taking Sumulong Highway. The intersection was frequently utilized, as many people
entering the National Capital Region, or going to Antipolo as well as Tanay via Marcos Highway.

Details of Interesection:
From Marikina via Marcos Highway:

  • Right: To Antipolo Proper via Sumulong Highway
  • Left: To Marikina via Sumulong Highway
  • Straight: To Infanta, Quezon via Marcos Highway

From Antipolo via Sumulong Highway:
  • Right: To Infanta, Quezon via Marcos Highway
  • Left: To Marikina via Marcos Highway
  • Straight: To Marikina via Sumulong Highway

From Marikina via Sumulong Highway:
  • Right: To Marikina via Marcos Highway
  • Left: To Infanta, Quezon via Marcos Highway (requires u-turn)
  • Straight: To Antipolo Proper via Sumulong Highway (requires u-turn)

From Infanta, Quezon via Marcos Highway:
  • Right: To Marikina via Sumulong Highway
  • Left: To Antipolo Proper via Sumulong Highway (requires u-turn)
  • Straight: To Marikina via Marcos Highway
