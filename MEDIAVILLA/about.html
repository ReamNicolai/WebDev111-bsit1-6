About the Author


Welcome, my guests! I am Mark Andrei T. Mediavilla, a first year BS Information Technology student of Our Lady of Fatima University (OLFU)-Antipolo
Campus. This is my startup website wherein I tried to apply what I learned from my subject Web Dev Design.

This website is all about my passion for travelling, videography, and most of all train driving thru train simulation. I love travelling to different places to
see the beauty around them. For the past years, I've been to differentplaces in Luzon and Visayas, discovering the tourist attractions everyone is talking about and familiarizing with the directions on how to get there.

There is one point in time during my high school days wherein I tried to document my provincial trip and shared it with social media. Unexpectedly, a
lot of people views the said clippings which gave me an idea that there are lots of people interested in road trips. This incident inspired me to video
cover my succeeding road trips and share some of it in social media.

My love for train driving can be considered as my first love because I am interested in every aspect of railway operations since childhood. I am
fascinated playing Thomas & Friends during my childhood days until I discover the train simulation game especially in European countries which I
play until now. I also learn to use some of the accessories in train simulation to make my own customed route.

Further, this website will share video coverage of some northern provincial trips to update the viewers of the current directions and what to expect
during road trips, and hopeful to cover other parts of the country in the near future. My train simulation in European countries will provide the viewers
with the location of actual train stations and how does the sceneries will look like. The viewers can download the videos and share with others but
please acknowledge.

Thank you for visiting my website!
